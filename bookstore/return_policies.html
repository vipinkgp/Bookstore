Return, Refund & Exchange Policy

Return

We accept returns within 5-7 days of order receipt by customers, subject to a quality check performed before final acceptance of the product return. Returns are not applicable for items purchased on sale.

Returns are accepted only when the received product is defective or damaged.

Product(s) being returned must be in their original condition. The packaging and tagging should be as received. Any defect in the product will incur a cost borne by the customer.

To request a return, write to us at vraj62023@gmail.com. Our team will respond within 72 hours on working days (Monday - Friday) between 10 AM and 6 PM.

To expedite the process, please include your order number, matter of concern, and, if possible, images of the product.

Your return order will be picked up within 3-4 working days (Monday - Friday) of your email.

Refund

Orders paid in cash can be refunded either as store credit or as a full refund to your bank account under the same name as the purchaser.

For orders placed using a card, we will reverse the transaction to the same card used for purchase. Refunds will take a minimum of 2-3 working days to reflect in your account/card.

Quality checks will be conducted on the returned product. We request you to allow time for the refund process to complete. Refunds will be processed only after the product is inspected and verified by our team.

Any misuse of our website, store items, or policies, including bad behavior or foul language, will not be tolerated.

Exchange

We accept exchanges within 7 days of order receipt by customers, subject to a quality check performed before final acceptance of the product exchange.

Product(s) being exchanged must be in their original condition and unwashed. The packaging and tagging should be as received. Any defect in the product will incur a cost borne by the customer.

To request an exchange, write to us at vraj62023@gmail.com, WhatsApp, or Instagram. Our team will respond within 72 hours on working days (Monday - Friday) between 10 AM and 6 PM.

Please include your order number and the matter of concern to help us expedite the process.

Your exchange order will be dispatched within 1-2 working days. If you have special requests during the exchange process, feel free to reach out to us.

As we do not cancel orders once they are placed, you may either return or exchange items as outlined above.
